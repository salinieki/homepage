Shortly before Midsummer, Salinieki travelled to the Irish town of Portlaoise
to take part in the 20th anniversary concert "(Pa) Saules Ceļu Izdejot" of the
dance group "Karbunkulis" on 20 June.

{{< figure
  src="images/foto/2026-06-karbunkulis/01-grupas-bilde.jpg"
  alt="Group photo of all the dance collectives at the Karbunkulis anniversary concert"
>}}

Three dance collectives took part in the concert — "Karbunkulis", "Rietumkrasts"
and "Salinieki" — along with the children's dance group and the Latvian choir.

{{< figure
  src="images/foto/2026-06-karbunkulis/06-iedzirkstities.jpg"
  alt="Salinieki dancing J. Purviņš's dance \"Iedzirkstīties\""
>}}

The theme of the concert was to dance through all four seasons of the year,
beginning and ending with Midsummer. So we opened the concert with J. Purviņš's
dance "Iedzirkstīties".

{{< figure
  src="images/foto/2026-06-karbunkulis/08-saule-brida-rudzu-lauku.jpg"
  alt="The group's girls dancing \"Saule brida rudzu lauku\""
>}}

Right after that the group's girls danced "Saule brida rudzu lauku". This was the
first time we performed this dance at a concert.

{{< figure
  src="images/foto/2026-06-karbunkulis/09-es-izjaju-prusu-zemi.jpg"
  alt="The group's boys dancing \"Es izjāju Prūšu zemi\""
>}}

And shortly afterwards it was the boys' turn to dance "Es izjāju Prūšu zemi".
This dance was also performed at a concert for the first time.

{{< figure
  src="images/foto/2026-06-karbunkulis/10-runu-raksti.jpg"
  alt="Salinieki dancing \"Rūnu raksti\""
>}}

In the second part of the concert, our group's first dance was "Rūnu raksti".

{{< figure
  src="images/foto/2026-06-karbunkulis/11-dzivibas-koks.jpg"
  alt="Salinieki dancing Aldis Salms's choreography \"Dzīvības koks\""
>}}

After that we danced Aldis Salms's choreography "Dzīvības koks".

We also danced "Ai bagāta man zemīte" and "Zeme zeļ, zeme plaukst" in this
concert. We closed the concert in a Midsummer mood with "Līgo danči" and
"Uguns rituāls".

{{< figure
  src="images/foto/2026-06-karbunkulis/02-salinieki-grupa.jpg"
  alt="Group photo of TDK \"Salinieki\""

Pāris", who helped us make up the number of boys in Ireland.
