The Latvian Independence Day concert took place at the St Columba's Church
hall. The concert featured the premiere of "Dzīvības koks" ("Tree of Life"),
a dance choreographed by our leader Aldis Salms.

{{< figure
  src="images/foto/2025-11-neatkaribas-diena/04-dzivibas-koks.jpg"
  alt="Salinieki perform the premiere of Aldis Salms' \"Dzīvības koks\" at the Latvian Independence Day concert in London"
>}}

At the concert we performed the dance with four couples, but the choreography
is designed to scale to different numbers of couples — particularly important
for diaspora groups.

We also danced "Buobu Dancis" and "Rūnu Raksti".

{{< figure
  src="images/foto/2025-11-neatkaribas-diena/02-runu-raksti.jpg"
  alt="Salinieki perform \"Rūnu Raksti\" at the Latvian Independence Day concert in London"
>}}

"Rūnu Raksti" was the closing dance of the concert, performed both on stage
and in the aisle.

{{< figure
  src="images/foto/2025-11-neatkaribas-diena/03-runu-raksti-2.jpg"
  alt="Salinieki dance the final dance \"Rūnu Raksti\" on stage and in the aisle, at the Latvian Independence Day concert in London"
>}}

The concert was filled with joy, warmth and genuine love for dance.
